Our Barbering Philosophy

Tradition meets modern craftsmanship

Founded in 2015, UpTown Barber was born from a passion for the traditional barbering arts combined with contemporary styling techniques. Our founder, James Wilson, envisioned a space where gentlemen could experience premium grooming in a relaxed, masculine environment.

We believe a great haircut is more than just a service - it's an experience that should leave you looking sharp and feeling confident. That's why we take the time to understand your personal style and grooming needs.
